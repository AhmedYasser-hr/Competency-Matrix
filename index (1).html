Core Competencies (All Employees)

Communication & Influence

Core

Interacting with others openly and effectively demonstrating accuracy, transparency, and clarity.

Behavioral Indicators by Level

1

Expresses perspective clearly; listens to others; uses appropriate body language.

2

Conveys meaning concisely; seeks to influence others; prevents arguments in discussions.

3

Conveys complex issues simply; balances speaking/listening; builds sustainable relationships.

4

Models persuasive strategies; drives cross-departmental communication; understands group dynamics.

Collaboration & Teamwork

Core

Communicating within and across groups to create alignment and share information.

Behavioral Indicators by Level

1

Shows respect; shares expertise; values input from others; encourages sharing views.

2

Communicates clear vision; creates sharing environment; works towards consensus-based solutions.

3

Facilitates cross-functional teams; removes barriers; fosters collaboration across teams.

4

Enhances outcomes through partnerships; drives fair culture; accessible despite seniority.
